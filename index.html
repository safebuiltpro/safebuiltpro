FAQ

Everything You
Need To Know

Are templates fully editable? +
Yes. Every template ships in Microsoft Word, Excel or PDF formats — fully unlocked. Edit, rebrand with your logo, and reuse across as many projects as you need.
Do I get lifetime access? +
Yes — once you purchase, you own that template pack forever. All future updates are included at no extra charge. Your download link never expires.
Are templates suitable for Nigerian construction? +
Absolutely. Templates are designed by a practising Nigerian geotechnical engineer and reference COREN, NESREA, and local standards — while also aligning with international best practices for cross-border projects.
What software do I need? +
Microsoft Word (for .docx files) and Microsoft Excel (for spreadsheet templates). Standard versions from Office 2016 onwards work perfectly. No special plugins or subscriptions needed.
Can my entire company use the templates? +
Yes. Templates are licensed for organisational use — deploy across teams, offices, and projects without purchasing additional licences. One purchase covers your entire firm.
Are updates included in my purchase? +
Yes. When standards change or we improve a template pack, existing customers receive updates at no extra cost. We notify you by email when your packs are updated.
